Gangsters Legacy

Discover the history of cult classic PC games that inspired the creation of this revolutionary mobile MMO

Gangsters (1992)

Revolutionary Strategy

The first game in the Gangsters series, released in 1992, became a true breakthrough in the strategy game genre. Developed by Hothouse Creations and published by Eidos Interactive, the game offered unique gameplay for managing a criminal empire.

Players took on the role of a crime boss who had to build their empire in a fictional American city of the 1920s. The game combined elements of strategy, economic simulation, and tactical combat.

Gangsters 2: Vendetta (1998)

Genre Evolution

Six years later, in 1998, the second part of the series was released - Gangsters 2: Vendetta. This game significantly improved the original gameplay by adding new mechanics and deeper development of game systems.

Gangsters 2 introduced improved graphics, a more detailed character management system, and expanded diplomacy options. The game received high ratings from critics and became a cult classic among strategy game fans.
